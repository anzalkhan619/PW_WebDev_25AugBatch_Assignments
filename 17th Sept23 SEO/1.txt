SEO stands for "Search Engine Optimization". It is the practice of designing and coding up your webpage in such a way that it
shows up in search engine's search results on as top as possible.

SEO is helpful because of the following reasons:
    . Increases the reach of your website.
    . Potentially increase revenue for your business.
    . Gain an edge on your business competitors (Better customer acquisition if your website shows on top of web search results).
