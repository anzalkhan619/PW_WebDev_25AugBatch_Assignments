Difference between relative and absolute positions:
    1. Relative positioned elements can be moved using top,right,bottom,left but their space is kept at their original positions.
       Absolute positioned elements lose their space at their original location.

    2. Relative positioned elements are not removed from the flow of the page
       Absolute positioned elements are removed from the usual flow of the page.

    3. Relative positioned element's default place is right where they have been placed.
       Absolute positioned elements take place relative to their closest parent or the html element itself.
